About Me

A short biography

Born and raised in Muskoka, ON., I developed an appreciation of nature by living there.

I obtained an advanced diploma in 'Game Development' at Durham College, as well as a diploma in 'Interactive Media Design-Web' at Georgian College, having achieved honors in both courses.

After graduating from Durham College, I was hired by Guru Studio in Toronto to produce textures and materials for the show 'Paw Patrol'. This taught me a lot about communication between team members, collaboration, troubleshooting, and the sharing of ideas. Production deadlines were very tight, and I had to learn to adapt my workflow to limit the amount of time spent on individual tasks in order to complete assignments on schedule.

I've since learned Web Development in Georgian College, and now seek to return to employment in the 3D Art field. It's much more rewarding to me to recreate or mimic real-world objects than web pages.

My other passions (in no particular order) include:

- Cooking
- Making electronic music
- Road trips
- Typography
- Casually playing MMOs
- Spicy food
- Biology

Thank you for taking the time to review my biography.
